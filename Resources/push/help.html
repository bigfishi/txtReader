  • How to transfer documents to TXT Reader
    1. Launch iTunes.
    2. Plug your device via USB.
    3. Select your device on the iTunes left pane.
    4. Select the Apps tab on the right pane.
    5. Scroll down to File Sharing.
    6. Select TXT Reader.
    7. Click on the "Addâ€¦" button or directly drag & drop documents on the list.
    8. Tap the "Refresh" / "Circle Arrow" button on your device.


    NOTE : It is impossible to copy folders and multiple selections from the computer to the device using this method.
